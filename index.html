About Comapny Profile

PAMCAL is a joint venture between PAMAC Finserve Pvt Ltd, India & Calculus Management Consultancy LLC, UAE. PAMAC is amongst the leading third party Process Management Companies in India providing services related to Credit, Risk and Operations for Banks, NBFC’s and Insurance Companies since last 25 years.

CALCULUS is a Leading service provider in UAE since last 30 years in the field of Legal Compliances and Advisory Services.
PAMCAL provides professional services to leading Clients across UAE in the area of Credit and Risk Checks since 2006.
